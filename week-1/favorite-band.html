Hozier

Hozier is an Irish singer-songwriter with a soulful voice and a poetic way of writing about love, faith, and the human experience. He first broke through with “Take Me to Church,” and his music since has kept that same mix of raw emotion and warmth that makes it feel both intimate and powerful.
